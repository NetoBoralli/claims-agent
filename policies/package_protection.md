# Package Protection Policy

## Eligibility window

A claim may only be filed after the package has exceeded its expected delivery window.

- Domestic ground: filing window opens **7 calendar days after the latest scan**, or **3 days after a "delivered" scan** for delivered-not-received cases.
- Express/2-day services: filing window opens **48 hours after the expected delivery date**.
- Any claim filed before the window opens is categorized as `not_yet_due` and must be denied with a friendly note pointing to the expected delivery date.

## Lost in transit

A claim is eligible as "lost in transit" when the carrier's last scan is older than 7 days and no delivery scan exists.

- Approve up to the **order subtotal**, capped at **$1,000 per claim**.
- Orders above $1,000 require human review (escalate).
- A $5 deductible applies to all approved refunds; refunds below $10 are issued as store credit.

## Delivered, not received ("porch piracy")

Claims where the tracking shows "delivered" but the customer reports non-receipt are higher risk.

- Auto-approve up to **$200** if the customer has zero prior claims in the last 90 days and the account is older than 30 days.
- Between **$200 and $500**: require an attached delivery photo, ring camera footage, or signed affidavit; escalate if missing.
- Above $500 or with any prior 90-day claim: always escalate.

## Damaged on arrival

A claim is eligible as "damaged" only when the customer provides photo evidence of the damage AND the carrier confirms delivery.

- With photos: approve up to **the value of the affected items** (not the full order).
- Without photos: deny with a request to reply with photos within 14 days.
- Repeated damage claims (>=2 in 90 days for the same customer) escalate regardless of evidence.

## Wrong item received

Wrong-item claims are forwarded to the merchant — Route covers only the shipping protection, not fulfillment errors.

- Always escalate so the merchant support queue handles the case.

## Refund mechanics

- Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within **3–5 business days**.
- Store-credit refunds are issued instantly and never expire.
- A claim, once decided, can only be reopened by a human agent.
